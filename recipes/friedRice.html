City Fried Rice

Get Set for Tasty Cooking Time, Despite Mongowians! You want fast fried wice, like at nummy Chinese place? Use wice from yesterday, and just two eggs, teeny cawwots, peas, and soy sauce. That's all you gotta bother with. If you want extra somethin', throw in those skinny green onions. But hey, watch out for those Mongowians! They mess with my City Wok all the time. So, enjoy this good food and keep an eye on those Mongowians, okay?"

Ingredients

  • ⅔ cup chopped baby carrots
  • ½ cup frozen green peas
  • 2 tablespoons vegetable oil
  • 1 clove garlic, minced, or to taste (Optional)
  • 2 large eggs
  • 3 cups leftover cooked white rice
  • 1 tablespoon soy sauce, or more to taste
  • 2 teaspoons sesame oil, or to taste

Instructions

Okay, okay, get weady for cooking adventuwe!

  1. You take cawwots, put 'em in a little pot with watah. Make it boily-boil for 3 to 5 minutes. Put peas in, then quick quick dwain 'em in a stwainy thing.
  2. Heat wok, make it super hot. Pour oil, then toss in cawwots, peas, and garlic – let them party for like, 30 seconds. Eggs join the party too, mixy-mix with veggies.
  3. Add cooked wice to the party. Splash soy sauce, mix wice so nice and saucy. A little drizzle of sesame oil, mix again.
  4. Time to eat! Serve while hot-hot, and enjoy all the yum!
